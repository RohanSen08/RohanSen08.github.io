Travel

Travelling is an amazing way to experience unique and different cultures around the world and meet new people. I am very grateful that I have had the opportunity to travel extensively since a young age. When I travel I try to spend time soaking in the unique culture and cuisine of wherever I may be, not just the tourist traps. I have visited over 20 countries all over the world and I believe that my experiences in each one has helped to shape me into an open-minded person.
