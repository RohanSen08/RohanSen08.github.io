I'm a student at Stuyvesant High School in NYC with a passion for coding, robotics, history, travel, and much more. I'm the captain of a world-level robotics team (#8721 Mighty Mechanics), a member of my school's NHS chapter, a ModelUN delegate for my school, a student at Columbia's SHP program, and an active participant in numerous clubs. In my free time, I teach the basics of coding to elementary school students, work on passion projects, participate in competitive coding competitions, and watch travel documentaries to learn more about the world.
